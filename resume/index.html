Software Development

  • swirlify, a comprehensive toolbox for swirl instructors // 2014 - Present
  • Website: http://swirlstats.com/instructors.html
  • GitHub: https://github.com/swirldev/swirl
  • swirlify allows anyone to create his or her own interactive content for the swirl educational platform, then to share that content with the world or with just a few people. The authoring tools accommodate instructor preference by allowing course authoring in multiple formats.
